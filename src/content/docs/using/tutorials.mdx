Hands-on walkthroughs that combine the [tools](/svgengine-site/using/tools/) and
[concepts](/svgengine-site/using/concepts/). Each one has an **interactive
playground** below the steps — try it right on the page, or in the full
[live demo](/svgengine-site/demo/).

## 1. Draw your first shape

1. Press `R` (or click the **Rectangle** in the tools palette).
2. **Drag** on the canvas to draw a rectangle. Hold **Shift** for a square, or
   **Alt** to draw from the center.
3. Press `V` to switch to **Select**, then click your shape.
4. In the **Inspector**, set a **fill** color. Done — your first object.

<PlaygroundEmbed route="draw-first-shape" />

## 2. Make it a rounded rectangle

1. Press `R` again.
2. In the **Tool Options** bar, raise **Corner radius**.
3. Drag a new rectangle — the corners are rounded.

<PlaygroundEmbed route="rounded-rectangle" />

## 3. Edit a path's points

1. Draw a shape, select it, and convert it to a **path**.
2. Press `A` for **Direct Select** — the anchor points appear.
3. **Drag an anchor** to move it; **double-click an anchor** to cycle its type
   (cusp ↔ smooth ↔ symmetric); **double-click a segment** to add a point.

<PlaygroundEmbed route="edit-path-points" />

## 4. Combine two shapes

1. Draw two overlapping shapes.
2. Press `V` and **Shift-click** both to select them.
3. Apply a **Pathfinder** operation — try **Union** to merge them, or
   **Subtract** to cut one from the other.

<PlaygroundEmbed route="combine-shapes" />

## 5. Draw a freehand stroke and clean it up

1. Press `P` for the **Pencil** and draw a freehand line.
2. Press `S` for **Smooth** and apply it to simplify the line.
3. Switch to **Direct Select** (`A`) for any final tweaks.

<PlaygroundEmbed route="freehand-smooth" />
